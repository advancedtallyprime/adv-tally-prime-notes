1. Theory – GST Ledger & Tax Rate Structure

CGST + SGST

Intra-State (Local) Supply
Maharashtra → Maharashtra

IGST

Inter-State Supply
Maharashtra → Gujarat / Goa

Input vs Output

Input GST → Paid on Purchase (ITC)
Output GST → Collected on Sales (Liability)

GST Rate Slabs

0% • 5% • 12% • 18% • 28%
