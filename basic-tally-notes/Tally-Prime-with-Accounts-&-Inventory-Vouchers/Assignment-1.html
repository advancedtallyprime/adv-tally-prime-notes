Voucher Types Overview

No Effect on Accounts

  • Sales Order → Intent to sell
  • Purchase Order → Intent to buy

Both Accounts + Inventory

  • Purchase Voucher
  • Sales Voucher
  • Debit Note (Purchase Return)
  • Credit Note (Sales Return)

Accounts Only

  • Payment Voucher (F5)
  • Receipt Voucher (F6)
  • Journal Voucher (F7)
  • Contra Voucher (F4)

Inventory Only

  • Delivery Note (Alt+F8)
  • Receipt Note (Alt+F9)
  • Rejection In (Alt+F6)
  • Rejection Out (Alt+F7)
  • Stock Journal
  • Physical Stock
