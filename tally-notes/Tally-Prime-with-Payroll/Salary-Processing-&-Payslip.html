The "Why" (Real-Life Context)

Day 5 is the "Grand Finale" where all your hard work comes together. In a real-world office, this happens on the last day of the month.

Think of this as the two-step checkout at a supermarket:

  1. Attendance: First, you scan the items (inputting the days worked).
  2. Payroll Voucher: Then, you hit "Total" (processing the salary).

Tally ensures that if an employee took unpaid leave, their Basic Pay, HRA, and PF are all reduced proportionally and automatically.

Step 1: Recording Attendance (The Input)

Before paying, you must tell Tally who was present and for how long.

  1. Go to Gateway of Tally → Vouchers.
  2. Press F10 (Other Vouchers) → Attendance.
  3. Press Ctrl + F (Autofill).
    • Process for: Attendance
    • Employee Group: All Items
    • Attendance Type: Present
  4. Enter Data: Refer to your assignment table (e.g., Rajesh: 25 Days, Priya: 26 Days).
  5. Repeat for Overtime: Record the hours for each employee (e.g., Rajesh: 10 Hrs).

Step 2: Processing Salary (The Calculation)

Now, we generate the accounting entry for the salary.

  1. Go to Vouchers → F10 → Payroll.
  2. Press Ctrl + F (Fill). This is the Payroll Auto-fill button.
    • Process For: Salary
    • From: 1-May-2021 | To: 31-May-2021
    • Employee Group: All Items
    • Payroll Ledger: Cash (or Bank)
  3. The Magic: Tally will instantly list all employees and calculate their Gross, Deductions, and Net Pay based on the rules you set on Day 4.

Step 3: Generating the Payslip (The Output)

A Payslip is the legal document given to an employee.

  1. Go to Gateway of Tally → Display More Reports → Payroll Reports → Payslip.
  2. Select Rajesh Saxena.

What you will see:

  • Earnings Side: Basic, HRA, DA, Bonus, Overtime
  • Deductions Side: PF and ESIC
  • Net Amount: The final figure after all math is done

Manual Calculation Check (The "Pro" Way)

As a Payroll Officer, you must always double-check one employee manually to ensure your "Rules" are correct.

Example: Rajesh Saxena

Basic Pay(20,000 / 31 days) × 25 days = ₹16,129.03
HRA (20% of Basic)16,129.03 × 0.20 = ₹3,225.81
PF (12% of Basic)16,129.03 × 0.12 = ₹1,935.48
