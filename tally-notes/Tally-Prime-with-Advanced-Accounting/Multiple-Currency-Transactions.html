1. Theory Section

1.1 What Is Multi-Currency Accounting?

Used when your business deals with foreign parties, banks, or currencies:

  • Export Sales in USD
  • Import Purchases in EUR
  • Foreign Bank Accounts (USD, GBP)
  • Automatic Forex Gain/Loss

1.3 How Tally Handles Currency

  • Base Currency: INR
  • Foreign currencies: USD, EUR, GBP, etc.
  • Exchange Rate per voucher date
  • All reports show: Foreign Amount + INR Equivalent
  • Auto Forex Gain/Loss on payment

1.5 Enable Multi-Currency

F11 ‚Üí Accounting Features ‚Üí
Maintain Multiple Currencies: YES
