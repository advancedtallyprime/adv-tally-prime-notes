1. Theory – Time of Supply (ToS) Rules

Advance Receipt (Sales)

Services: GST payable on advance
Goods: Exempt from tax on advance (as of 2019)

Advance Payment (Purchases)

RCM applicable on advance paid to URD or for Import of Services
→ Pay IGST/CGST+SGST + Claim ITC

2. Practical Setup – Must Enable This!

F11 → Statutory & Taxation → Set/Alter GST Details
→ Enable Tax Liability on Advance Receipts → YES

Without this, Tally will NOT trigger advance tax calculation!

3. Step-by-Step Entries

Scenario A: Advance Receipt ₹5,000 (AMC Service)

Taxable Value = 5,000 ÷ 1.18 ≈ ₹4,237.29 | GST ≈ ₹762.71

1. Receipt Voucher (F6)
Bank Dr ₹5,000 → Local Customer Cr
2. Increase Liability (F7 + Stat Adjustment)
→ Nature: Increase in Tax Liability – Advance Receipts
Dr Advance Tax Paid (Current Asset) → ₹762.71
Cr Output CGST → ₹381.36 | Cr Output SGST → ₹381.36
3. Final Sales Invoice (₹10,000 + ₹1,800 GST)
Use “Agst Ref” → Adjust ₹5,000 advance
4. Reverse Liability (F7 + Stat Adjustment)
→ Nature: Decrease in Tax Liability – Sales against Advance
Dr Output CGST/SGST → Cr Advance Tax Paid

Scenario B: Advance Payment to URD (Legal Service ₹3,000)

1. Payment Voucher (F5) → Bank Cr → URD Supplier Dr
2. RCM Liability Journal → Output CGST/SGST Dr → Taxes Payable RCM Cr
3. ITC Claim Journal → Input CGST/SGST Dr → Taxes Payable RCM Cr

4. Reports – GSTR-1 & GSTR-3B

  • GSTR-1: Table 11A → Advance received (adjusted later in 11B)
  • GSTR-3B: Table 3.1(a) → Includes advance tax liability

6. Assignment – ₹23,600 Advance Receipt (GST Inclusive)

Transaction: ₹23,600 received on 01-Aug-2024 for AMC Service

  1. Calculate:
    Taxable Value = 23,600 ÷ 1.18 = ₹20,000
    GST = ₹3,600 (₹1,800 CGST + ₹1,800 SGST)
  2. Record Receipt Voucher → ₹23,600
  3. Journal: Increase Tax Liability → Dr Advance Tax Paid ₹3,600 → Cr Output CGST/SGST
  4. September: Raise Sales Invoice ₹20,000 + ₹3,600 GST
    → Adjust full ₹23,600 advance
  5. Reverse Liability Journal → Clear Advance Tax Paid
  6. Goods Check: If this was Laptop advance → No liability journal
