1. Theory – Reverse Charge Mechanism (RCM)

Standard (Forward Charge)

Supplier charges & collects GST
Recipient pays to supplier

RCM (Reverse Charge)

Recipient calculates & pays GST directly to govt
→ Claim ITC in same month

When RCM Applies

  • Specific Services: GTA, Legal, Security, Sponsorship
  • URD Purchases (Notified Goods/Services)
  • Import of Services

Net Effect: Revenue-Neutral (Liability = ITC)

2. Practical Setup – URD Supplier & RCM Config

Create URD Supplier Ledger

Name: Local URD Supplier
Under: Sundry Creditors
GST Applicable: Applicable → Set/Alter GST Details
Registration Type: Unregistered
State: Maharashtra

Enable RCM for Service (Legal Fees)

Alter → Legal Fees Ledger → Set/Alter GST Details
→ Is Reverse Charge Applicable? → Yes
→ Rate: 18%

3. Step-by-Step Entries – Legal Services ₹10,000 (RCM)

1. Purchase Voucher (F9)
Party: Local URD Supplier
Legal Fees Dr → ₹10,000
No GST (URD doesn't charge)
2. RCM Liability Journal (F7 + Alt+J)
→ Type: GST → Increase in Tax Liability → URD Purchase
Output CGST Dr ₹900 + Output SGST Dr ₹900
Cr Taxes Payable RCM → ₹1,800
3. ITC Claim Journal (F7 + Alt+J)
→ Type: GST → Increase of Input Tax Credit → URD Purchase
Input CGST Dr ₹900 + Input SGST Dr ₹900
Cr Taxes Payable RCM → ₹1,800
Net Zero!
