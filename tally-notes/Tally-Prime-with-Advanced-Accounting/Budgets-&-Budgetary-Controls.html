1. Theory – Complete Explanation

1.1 What Are Budgets in Tally Prime?

A financial plan that controls income, expenses, and project costs.

  • Ledger-wise Budget (e.g., Advertising ₹60,000/year)
  • Group-wise Budget (Indirect Expenses ₹8L)
  • Cost Centre Budget (Project Alpha ₹2L)
  • Real-time Actual vs Budget comparison

1.3 Budget Types Supported

TypeDescription
Periodic BudgetMonthly / Quarterly / Yearly
Ledger BudgetSpecific ledger control
Group BudgetEntire group (e.g., Indirect Expenses)
Cost Centre BudgetProject / Department limits
On Net TransactionsIgnores opening balance
