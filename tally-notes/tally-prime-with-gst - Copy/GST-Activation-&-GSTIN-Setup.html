1. Theory – Understanding GST Framework

GST

Goods & Services Tax
Unified indirect tax on supply of goods & services across India

GSTIN (15-digit Unique ID)

Example: 27ABCDE1234F1Z5

  • 27 → Maharashtra State Code
  • ABCDE1234F → PAN-based
  • 1 → Entity count
  • Z5 → Check digit

Mandatory for all registered businesses

HSN & SAC Codes

HSN: For Goods (e.g., 8517 → Mobile Phones)
SAC: For Services (e.g., 998311 → Management Consulting)

Required for correct tax rates & GSTR filing

2. Practical Setup – Enable GST in Tally Prime

Step 1: Enable GST Features

Gateway of Tally → F11 (Company Features) → Statutory & Taxation

  • Enable Goods and Services Tax (GST) → Yes
  • Set/Alter GST Details → Yes
