1. Theory – Import of Goods & Services

Import of Goods

Treated as Inter-State Supply → IGST Applicable
IGST calculated on (Assessable Value + BCD)
ITC Fully Available

Import of Services

Reverse Charge Mechanism (RCM)
You pay IGST → Claim ITC in same month
Cash Neutral Transaction

6. Assignment – High BCD Import (Mouse)

Assessable Value: ₹40,000 | BCD: 20% | IGST: 18%

  1. Calculate:
    • BCD = ₹8,000
    • Value for IGST = ₹48,000
    • IGST = ₹8,640
  2. Record Purchase Voucher with:
    • Duty Ledger → ₹8,000
    • Input IGST → ₹8,640
  3. ITC Claimable in: May GSTR-3B (if BOE valid)
  4. Verify Input IGST Ledger shows credit of ₹8,640
